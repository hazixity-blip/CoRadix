The CoRadix Method

Three pillars.
One clear path.

Most students study everything the same way. We don't. Every topic is deliberately sorted into three distinct modes so your brain processes it the right way.

01

Memorise

Dates, definitions, formulas — content that needs to live in long-term memory. We use spaced repetition, mnemonics, and visual anchors to make it stick without cramming.

Spaced RepetitionMnemonicsVisual Anchors
02

Understand

Conceptual content needs depth, not drilling. Socratic questioning, analogy-building, and first-principles explanations so students genuinely grasp the "why" behind every topic.

First PrinciplesAnalogiesSocratic Q&A
03

Practise

Application is learnt by doing — not watching. Targeted, progressive problems that close the gap between understanding and performing under exam pressure.

Targeted ProblemsExam SimulationFeedback Loops

Materials & Access Policy

Your materials are
yours — protect them.

Every resource we share is created exclusively for enrolled students. Here's how we keep materials secure, and what that means for you.

All notes, worksheets, recordings, and resources shared through CoRadix Academy are the intellectual property of CoRadix Academy. They are licensed for the personal, non-commercial use of the enrolled student only.

🚫

No Sharing or Forwarding

Materials must not be shared with anyone outside the enrolled student — not friends, not other students, not online groups.

📵

No Screenshots of Session Content

Screenshots or screen-recordings of live sessions, shared screens, or whiteboards are strictly prohibited without written permission.

⬇️

No Downloading or Redistribution

Files shared via Google Drive are view-only. Downloading, printing for distribution, or re-uploading anywhere is not permitted.

⚖️

Legal Consequences Apply

Redistribution of CoRadix materials is a violation of the Terms & Conditions and may be pursued legally. Read the full T&C →

🔖 All materials are watermarked. Every document and resource is stamped with the student's name and ID. If a file is found circulating, we will know exactly where it came from.
🔐

How Material Access Works

Secure, simple, and personal

1
Once enrolled, every student is assigned a unique Student ID (e.g. CRX-2026-001).
2
A private Google Drive folder is created for your child and shared only with your registered email address.
3
Access is view-only by default — files cannot be downloaded or copied directly from the Drive.
4
When a student leaves the academy, access is revoked immediately with one click.
5
If you ever lose access or change your email, contact us at academy@coradix.academy to update your details.
Your Student ID is your reference for everything — sessions, materials, tasks, and communications. Keep it safe. You'll receive it by email upon enrollment confirmation.

CoRadix was founded by Sam — a biotech and psychology student who realised most students struggle not because they lack intelligence, but because no one ever showed them how to learn.

"I built the method I wish someone had handed me in school. Every student deserves to know what to memorise, what to understand, and what to practise — and to feel the difference."
