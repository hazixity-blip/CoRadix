These Terms & Conditions govern the enrollment of students into CoRadix Academy's programs. By submitting the enrollment form and checking the agreement box, the parent or legal guardian confirms they have read, understood, and agreed to all terms below on behalf of themselves and the enrolled student.

1 Batch Rules & Attendance Policy

  • Each batch is capped at a maximum of 9 students. Enrollment is confirmed on a first-come, first-served basis upon receipt of payment.
  • Students are expected to attend all scheduled sessions on time. Punctuality is part of the learning commitment.
  • If a student is unable to attend a session, prior notice must be given. Habitual absenteeism without notice may result in the student's place being offered to a waitlisted student.
  • No session will be rescheduled, compromised, or cancelled on account of an individual student's personal schedule — including exam periods. The batch runs as scheduled for all.
  • During exam seasons, CoRadix may conduct open Zoom revision sessions — live, interactive doubt-clearing classes open to all enrolled students. Students can ask questions and interact with one another under the direct supervision of the founder. These are offered as additional support, not as replacements for regular sessions.
1-on-1 Sessions: Standard enrollment is for group batches (up to 9 students). Parents who require dedicated 1-on-1 sessions for their child may request this separately. 1-on-1 sessions are available at an additional fee, subject to slot availability. Please enquire at the time of enrollment.

2 Fee Payment & Refund Policy

  • All fees are due and must be paid in full at the beginning of each month, before the month's sessions commence.
  • Failure to pay by the due date may result in the student's place being temporarily suspended until payment is cleared.
  • Fees are non-refundable once the month has begun, regardless of attendance or personal circumstances.
  • If a student wishes to discontinue, notice must be given before the next monthly payment cycle. No refund will be issued for the current month.
  • CoRadix Academy reserves the right to revise fees at the start of a new academic term, with at least 2 weeks' prior notice to enrolled families.
⚠️ Fees not paid by the start of the month may result in the student's seat being reassigned to a waitlisted applicant without further notice.

3 Post-Class Task Policy

CoRadix assigns one task per session to consolidate the material covered. This is a core part of the learning methodology and is not optional.

  • Every task must be submitted within 30 minutes of the session ending — no exceptions.
  • Tasks are concise and directly tied to what was covered in that session. They are not lengthy homework assignments.
  • Consistent failure to submit tasks on time will be flagged and discussed with the parent/guardian.
  • Late or missing submissions are marked incomplete — they do not carry over to the next session.
Why 30 minutes? Submitting immediately after class consolidates memory at its peak. This is a deliberate part of the CoRadix method — not an arbitrary rule.

4 Code of Conduct for Students

CoRadix is a small, focused learning environment. All students are expected to uphold the following:

  • Treat fellow students, the tutor, and the shared learning space with respect at all times.
  • Arrive to sessions prepared — materials ready, distractions minimised.
  • Active participation is encouraged. Passive attendance (camera off, no interaction) is discouraged and may be flagged.
  • Any form of disruptive behaviour, bullying, or disrespect — in sessions or in community subject rooms — will result in a warning, and on repetition, removal from the batch without refund.
  • Subject community rooms are for academic discussion only. Misuse of these spaces will result in access being revoked.

5 Photo & Content Usage Consent

  • Sessions may occasionally be recorded by the tutor for internal quality review only. Recordings will not be shared publicly.
  • Student work, anonymised feedback, or progress highlights may be shared on CoRadix's social media or website for promotional purposes only with prior written consent from the parent/guardian.
  • Students and parents must not record, screenshot, or distribute session content without explicit written permission from CoRadix Academy.
  • Any content — notes, worksheets, or materials — shared by CoRadix is for the enrolled student's personal use only and may not be redistributed.

6 Privacy & Data Policy

  • Personal information collected during enrollment is used solely for managing the student's enrollment and communication with the parent/guardian.
  • CoRadix Academy does not sell, share, or distribute personal data to any third party.
  • All data is stored securely and handled responsibly.
  • Parents may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting the academy directly.

7 General

  • CoRadix Academy reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Changes will be communicated to enrolled families with reasonable notice.
  • CoRadix Academy reserves the right to decline or terminate enrollment if these terms are repeatedly violated.
  • For any questions regarding these terms, contact us at academy@coradix.academy.
